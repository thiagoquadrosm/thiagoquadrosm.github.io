webstories

nexo jornal — 2021

Webstory is Google's new format for mobile-optimized editorial content. It allows for more comfortable browsing and an interactive and immersive experience. Webstory content has greater visual appeal and has a variety of resources, such as text, images, videos, animations and interactive components.

I was responsible for developing the visual language of Nexo Jornal's webstories, adapting different types of content already published in the newspaper to the new format.

Webstories are built on Makestories, a new tool with several limitations. The challenge was to maintain visual coherence with the newspaper's visual identity. At the same time, make them attractive in the language that asks for the format, for an audience that is not the captive of Nexo.
