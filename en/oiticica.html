the multiple meanings of helio oiticica's art

nexo jornal — 2020

The special "The multiple meanings of the art of Hélio Oiticica" is a retrospective of the work and trajectory of one of the most important Brazilian artists. With an introductory text, critical comments and presentations of his main works, the special article condenses Oiticica's work not only in content but also in form.

Inspired by the artist's work, the layout was designed in a three-dimensional way, with images and texts positioned on different planes — this effect was developed in css using 3D parallax. The images slide along with the text and large colorful shapes, the content invites the reader to enter this environment and interact (virtually) with its images.

parallax 3D

The parallax effect was developed using a perspective technique — a three-dimensional space is created on the page, and elements are distributed in 8 layers, from closest to farthest from the screen's point of view.

Elements placed on the last layer move more slowly relative to the movement of the page. On the other hand, they are also displayed in a much smaller size.

By resizing these elements so that they are proportionate in size to those of the closest layers, the effect is one of sliding and sinking — and a smooth impression of depth.

This project was developed using the Visual Studio Code, with joint layout and direction by Guilherme Falcão
