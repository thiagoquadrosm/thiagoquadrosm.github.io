cientists from brazil you need to know

nexo jornal — 2020-2021

"Scientists from Brazil you need to know" is a scientific dissemination project by Nexo in partnership with the Serrapilheira Institute. Still in progress, the project is formed by several fronts for the production of content about Brazilian scientists, both historical and active.

visual identity

As it is a special project, a visual identity was developed to identify and unify the different contents of the project. The main element, present in all project materials, are colors — trios of colors that must always be used exclusively, that is, a trio for content.

Set of four color palettes with three colors each, formed by multiplication

Through three floating hexagons, the background of the illustrations is constructed, as well as the visual elements of the identity. These polygons overlap and shift, also functioning as a transition between scenes.

Storyboard of the opening of the videos, with the brand of Nexo and the series

videos

12 videos were produced about important scientists in the history of Brazil, their work and their contributions to the evolution of science. I developed the visual identity and most of the illustrations for the videos — later animated by the newspaper's audiovisual team.

The goal was to produce a drawing style that was quick and easy to animate, but at the same time maintain movement on the screen, as the main element of the video is the narration. In addition, a versatile trait was needed that could adapt to illustrative and schematic drawings on different areas of knowledge: from physics to agronomy.

special articles and podcasts

In the second part of the project, podcasts of interviews are being produced with great names in Brazilian science today. Each podcast is accompanied by a special article, with the interview transcript, photos and illustrations. I was responsible for the graphic design and programming of the pages.
